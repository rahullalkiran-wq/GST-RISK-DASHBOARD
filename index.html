Override Rules (Hard Floors)
Suo-Moto Cancellation
≥ 90
Hard floor — state forced closure. Score cannot fall below 90. ML score can still go higher.
Voluntary Cancellation
≥ 85
Hard floor — taxpayer-initiated closure. Slightly less severe than suo-moto.
Suspended Status
≥ 70
Hard floor — entity under regulatory scrutiny. Cannot fall below 70.
Risk Band Thresholds & Examples
Low Risk
0 – 20
Active status, Yellow/Green compliance, regular filings within 3 months, no batch filing or divergence signals.
Example: JAGABANDHU MOHAPATRA (Score: 5)
Active · Yellow · Filed Mar 2026 · No flags → Lend with standard terms
Medium Risk
21 – 45
Active but some filing lag (4–12 months) or Orange compliance. May have one flag. Monitor quarterly.
Example: HARAGOURI INDUSTRIES (Score: 43)
Active · Yellow · 8-month gap · Batch + Divergence → Enhanced monitoring
High Risk
46 – 70
Active but Red compliance, significant filing gaps (13–24 months), batch filing detected, or multiple flags present.
Example: RAJA PRADHAN (Score: 53)
Active · Red · 15-month gap · Batch filing → Collateral required
Critical
71 – 100+
Cancelled (suo-moto or voluntary), Black/Red compliance, years of silence, override floor applies. Do not lend.
Example: JAGANNATH TRADERS (Score: 108)
Cancelled S.M · Black · 56-month gap · All 5 flags → Reject
